Fire Roasted Tomato Chicken Pasta

An orzo based pasta dish with fire roasted tomatoes, parmesan, and organic chicken thighs. Garnished with flatleaf parsley.

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Season the chicken with salt and pepper on both sides, and add to the skillet. Sear on both sides for 8 minutes total.
  2. Add the fire roasted tomato sauce and simmer on medium-low for 15 minutes.
  3. Shred the chicken with two forks right in the skillet.
  4. In the meantime, cook the orzo in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Drain and stir into the chicken.
  5. Off the heat add the parmesan and stir to combine. Add another pinch of salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately!
